## 8.1 Removing, packing, loading

To remove the Hand Washing Station, proceed as follows:

<Steps>
  <Step>Turn off the water supply.</Step>
  <Step>Activate the cleaning mode button to release water in the pipes.</Step>
  <Step>
    Remove the soap bag and paper towels from the Hand Washing Station.
  </Step>
  <Step>Flip the power switch to "Off".</Step>
  <Step>
    Disconnect the power supply from power mains and disconnect the power supply
    from the Hand Washing Station.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Disconnect the water supply and drain connectors. Use a bucket to dispose of
    remaining water in the pipes.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Remove the screws used to fix the Hand Washing Station to the wall bracket
    or the counter
  </Step>
  <Step>
    Remove the Hand Washing Station from the wall bracket or the counter.
  </Step>
  <Step>
    In case of Wall-mounted model: Remove the wall bracket from the wall.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout>
  Always use the original packaging to pack the Hand Washing Station and the
  other components to protect them from damage.
</Callout>

## 8.2 Transport

<Callout type="warning">
  Avoid any shock, significant vibrations and damage to the Hand Washing Station
  during transport. Always pack the system in its original packaging.
</Callout>

## 8.4 Environmental protection and disposal

<Callout type="info">
  Dispose of packaging material and old equipment in compliance with relevant safety and environmental regulations. When the Hand Washing Station becomes outdated and has to be decommissioned, the applicable legal provisions and regulations regarding disposal must be observed.

It is important to dispose of recyclable materials properly and with consideration given to our environment. Please contact your waste disposal center if you are in any doubt.

</Callout>

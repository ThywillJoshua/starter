A Wall-mounted version
The Hand Washing Station provides a complete hand wash cycle.

<accordion title="Wetting and soaping">
  The SMU mixes water, soap and air to a soap emulsion enabling efficient
  wetting and soaping in one step. The duration of soap distribution as well as
  the amount of soap is configurable.
  <callout>
    The factory setting is 0.5 ml of soap, which is dispensed during 3 seconds.
  </callout>
</accordion>
<accordion title="Pause">
  The configurable pause between the steps wetting / soaping and rinsing allows
  additional scrubbing time.
</accordion>
<accordion title="Rinsing">
  Hands are rinsed with a water and air mixture. The rinsing time is
  configurable.
</accordion>
<accordion title="Drying">
  The paper dispenser provides paper for drying hands. The Wall-mounted and
  Free-standing models additionally include a paper bin.
</accordion>

## 3.2 Washing hands

<steps>

### Approach the Station

A body sensor detects the presence of a person in front of the Hand Washing Station. Once a person is detected, a white light illuminates the sink.

### Start the Cycle

As soon as the user puts his/her hands under the outlet, a hand sensor initiates a hand-washing cycle.

## 3.3 SMU and Docking Station

The Smart Mixing Unit (SMU) and Docking Station are the key components for the hand washing function.

<accordion title="Smart Mixing Unit (SMU)">
The SMU performs the following functions:
- dosing and pumping the soap into the mixing chamber
- mixing soap, water and air to a soap emulsion
- dispensing the soap emulsion for the wetting and soaping step as well as distribution of a mixture of water and air for the rinsing step

<accordion title="Docking Station">
The Docking Station is the fixture of the SMU. It comprises:
- motor of the soap pump
- quick connection with the water line
<callout>
To open the docking station, just pull in your direction. To close the docking station, push it back into place until it snaps with a clicking sound.
</callout>
</accordion>

Exporting your results is effortless. After you’ve completed the survey, you can export them at any time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Work with results">
    On the **Results** page, click **Work with results** under your name (top-right).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download guides">
    Choose **Download feedback discussion guide** and **Results analysis guide**; they include your results and facilitation tips.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="note" title="Any time">
  You can export as often as you need.
</Callout>

---
title: Rating visibility
description: Clarifies how rating visibility works when sharing Peer Feedback results.
icon: message-circle
---

[Source](https://www.start-pulse.com/en/resources/help/peer-feedback/feedback-visibility-and-teams/who-can-see-my-rating)

The rating is only visible to feedback seekers (the peson sending out a peer feedback survey). They can only see which of the three categories - Improve (1 to 6), Neutral ( 7 & 8), Continue (8 & 9) - a response belongs to by filtering the comments by category.

For more information, see: [Who sees my feedback?](https://www.start-pulse.com/en/resources/help/peer-feedback/who-sees-my-feedback)

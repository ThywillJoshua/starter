title: Answer visibility duration
description: Clarifies how answer visibility duration works when sharing Peer Feedback results.
icon: message-circle
---

[Source](https://www.start-pulse.com/en/resources/help/peer-feedback/feedback-visibility-and-teams/how-long-can-my-answers-be-viewed)

The survey's data is stored as long as your company uses Pulse. This ensures that answers to the different questions can be compared with those of previous surveys. However, your company may impose a time limit (e.g., anonymise all comments after 18 months).

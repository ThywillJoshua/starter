title: See who has participated in your survey
description: Clarifies how to see who has participated in your survey when sharing Peer Feedback results.
icon: message-circle
---

[Source](https://www.start-pulse.com/en/resources/help/peer-feedback/feedback-visibility-and-teams/how-can-i-see-who-has-participated-in-my-survey)

You can see who took part in the survey in the **Dashboard**. In addition, the people who gave feedback are shown in the **results** when you move the mouse pointer over the round person icons or tap on them in the mobile app.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check dashboard">
    Open the **Dashboard** to see who has participated.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Hover/tap in results">
    In results, hover over or tap the round person icons to see who gave feedback.
  </Step>
</Steps>

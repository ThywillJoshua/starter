These aspects will help you to make the outcome discussion with your peers a success.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Warm up the room">
    Start with a check-in so peers feel comfortable and ready to discuss the results.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Discuss feedback">
    Walk through key themes, invite questions, and keep the tone constructive.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Close with reflection">
    Share what went well and what to improve for next time; each person can reflect briefly.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download guides">
    In **Results**, click **Work with the results** (top-right) → download the **Feedback Conversation Guide** and **Results Analysis and Reflection Guide** to structure the discussion.
  </Step>
</Steps>

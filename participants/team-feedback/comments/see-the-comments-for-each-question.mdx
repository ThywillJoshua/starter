[Source](https://www.start-pulse.com/en/resources/help/team-feedback/comments/how-can-i-see-the-comments-for-each-question)

A rating must be given for each question. Participants can voluntarily add a comment while answering the question to further explain the ratings.

<Callout variant="info" title="Before you start">
  You must give a rating for each question to submit the survey; comments are optional but encouraged.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Results">
    Go to the **Results** page and find the question you want.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Show details">
    Click **Show details** on that question to reveal comments.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse comments">
    Scroll to read all comments for that question; use the arrow in the upper-right to move to the next question’s comments.
  </Step>
</Steps>

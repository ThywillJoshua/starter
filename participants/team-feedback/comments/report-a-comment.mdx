If you come across a comment that you deem insulting, discriminatory or otherwise problematic, you have the possibility to report it to your company's Pulse team.

<Callout variant="info" title="When to report">
  Report comments that are insulting, discriminatory, or otherwise inappropriate.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Flag the comment">
    Click the **flag** icon on the upper-right of the comment.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm report">
    In the pop-up, click **Report comment**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add context">
    Provide the reason and click **Submit**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="warning" title="Misuse">
  Abusive or unlawful comments may be removed; misuse of reporting can result in disciplinary action.
</Callout>

If you come across a comment that you deem insulting, discriminatory or otherwise problematic, you have the possibility to report it to your company's Pulse team.

**Reporting a comment**

1. Click on the **flag icon** located on the right corner of the comment.
2. A pop-up window will open, and you can click on **Report comment**.
3. Specify the reasons for reporting this comment and click on **Submit**.

**Note:** Creaholic and your company have no control over the scores and comments that are provided. However, it reserves the right to remove comments, especially the abusive or unlawful ones. Misuse may result in disciplinary action and other measures.

Liking comments is a quick way to show your agreement with a topic mentioned in your colleagues’ comments.

<Callout variant="info" title="What likes do">
  Signal agreement without adding another comment.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Like a comment">
    Click the **Like** icon at the bottom of a comment.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="warning" title="Visibility">
  Same rules as comments: teammates can see you liked it; others see likes anonymously.
</Callout>

With numerous employees, there may be many comments. To get an overview as quickly as possible, various filtering options are available to you. Filter the comments by language, most frequently mentioned topics and by rating (Improve, Neutral, Keep it up).

<Callout variant="info" title="Filter options">
  Language, most-mentioned topics, and rating buckets (Improve, Neutral, Keep it up).
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open question details">
    On the results page, click **Show details** for the question you want.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter by language/topic">
    Under the score bar, choose a language filter and/or a suggested topic from text analysis.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Filter by rating">
    Click a rating bucket (Red = Improve, Grey = Neutral, Green = Keep it up). You can select multiple buckets.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Clear filters">
    Above the comments, see active filters. Click **Delete filters** or remove individual filters.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="warning" title="Narrowing results">
  Multiple filters combine; more criteria mean fewer comments shown.
</Callout>

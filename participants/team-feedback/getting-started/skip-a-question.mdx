<Callout variant="info" title="When to skip">
  Skipping is available for both ratings and comments. Use it sparingly—complete answers improve team insights and actionability.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the question">
    Scroll to the item you do not want to rate or comment on.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Skip question">
    Below the comment fields, select **Skip question**. The item will be marked as skipped and you can continue with the survey.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Return later (optional)">
    You can revisit a skipped item before submitting the survey if you change your mind.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="warning" title="Impacts of skipping">
  Skipped items are excluded from your responses and may reduce the completeness of team results. Encourage the survey creator to refine questions that feel hard to answer.
</Callout>

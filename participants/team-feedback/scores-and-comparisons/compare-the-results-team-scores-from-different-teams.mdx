You can view the results of all teams in your company. With the comparison function, you can easily compare teams at all levels of the hierarchy.

1. Click on the corresponding survey in the **Dashboard**. 2\. click on the **Plus sign** on the results page.
2. Click on the **Plus sign** on the results page. This is located above the score bars on the left. 3.
3. Select the teams and click on **Apply**
4. You can now compare the scores of the selected teams directly with each other.

**Good to know**: You can also create your own groups here and include them in the comparison.

[Source](https://www.start-pulse.com/en/resources/help/team-feedback/scores-and-comparisons/how-is-the-score-calculated)

The scores for each question are assigned to one of three possible categories: Improve (1 to 6), Neutral (7 & 8) and Keep it up (9 & 10).

**The score is calculated as follows**

The percentage of participants in "Keep it up" group, to which the percentage of participants in the "Improve" group is deducted (scores are presented without "%"). The lowest possible score is -100, the highest possible +100.

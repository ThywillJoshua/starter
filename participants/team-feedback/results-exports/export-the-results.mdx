Exporting your results is easy. After completing the survey, you can export the results at any time.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Work with results">
    In **Results**, click **Work with results** near your name (top-right).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose export">
    Select **Export as PDF**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick scope">
    Export the current page or all results (overview + details for selected teams; up to 50 comments per question).
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="note" title="Any time">
  You can export as often as you need.
</Callout>

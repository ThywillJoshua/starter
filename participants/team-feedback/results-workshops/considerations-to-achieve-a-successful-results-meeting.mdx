These aspects will help you to successfully lead the discussion about the results with your team.

<Steps>
  <Step title="Warm up the room">
    Start with a check-in (how people feel/what’s on their mind) or buzz groups to set an open tone.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set meeting principles">
    Align on basics (e.g., active listening, raising hands if principles aren’t followed).
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review results together">
    Walk through the key findings; invite reactions and questions.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Close with reflection">
    Run a check-out: what went well and what to improve for next time.
  </Step>
</Steps>

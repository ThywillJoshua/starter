The Pulse team of your company may decide to ask questions solely to the teams affected by them.

Find out how to direct questions to specific people [here](https://www.start-pulse.com/en/resources/help/managing-surveys/how-can-i-assign-a-survey-to-selected-teams-only)

<Callout variant="info" title="Targeted questions">
  Some questions only appear for teams they apply to, keeping surveys shorter and more relevant.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="warning" title="What you see">
  If a question doesn’t apply to your team, it won’t appear in your survey.
</Callout>

The more your feedback is concrete and precise, the easier it'll be for your colleagues to understand it, to discuss and implement the appropriate measures. [You'll find important tips to help you formulate your feedback here](https://www.start-pulse.com/en/resources/how-do-i-give-feedback).

<Callout variant="info" title="Make it actionable">
  Be specific, cite examples, and note impacts so teammates can use your feedback.
</Callout>

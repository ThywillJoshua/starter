description: Advice for respondents on how to fix incorrect team assignments.

---

[Source](https://www.start-pulse.com/en/resources/help/team-feedback/giving-feedback/my-team-is-no-longer-correct-what-can-i-do)

Report it to your company's Pulse Team. They'll help you as soon as possible.

<Callout variant="info" title="Why it matters">
  Wrong team assignments affect who sees your feedback and which surveys you get.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Contact your admin or Pulse team">
    Report the wrong team/manager mapping so they can update it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm the fix">
    Ask to be re-assigned and verify in your next survey that the team is correct.
  </Step>
</Steps>

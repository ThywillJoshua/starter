Participation in surveys, i.e., answering and commenting on questions, is usually optional. Employees who don't participate shouldn't be disadvantaged in any way.

<Callout variant="info" title="Participation stance">
  Participation is typically voluntary; non-participation should not lead to disadvantages.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="warning" title="Why participate anyway">
  More participation leads to better team insights and more actionable results.
</Callout>

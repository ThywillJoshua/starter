[Source](https://www.start-pulse.com/en/resources/help/team-feedback/giving-feedback/do-i-have-to-participate-in-the-surveys)

Participation in surveys, i.e., answering and commenting on questions, is usually optional. Employees who don't participate shouldn't be disadvantaged in any way.

<Callout variant="info" title="Why add a photo">
  A clear profile image makes it easier for teammates to recognize your comments in results, where visibility rules allow.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your profile">
    Click your name in the top-right corner, then select **Profile**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Upload a photo">
    Click the profile picture box and choose an image from your device.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Resize and save">
    Adjust the crop if needed, then click **Save**.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="warning" title="Visibility">
  Your profile photo is only shown to people who are allowed to see your feedback, based on survey visibility rules.
</Callout>

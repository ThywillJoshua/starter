<Callout variant="info" title="Available languages">
  Pulse supports German, French, Italian, and English. Your company may restrict which ones are shown.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the language switcher">
    Scroll to the bottom-left corner of the page to see the language list.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Select your language">
    Click a language. The active option becomes underlined and the UI switches immediately.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify survey text">
    If a survey lacks translations, some items may stay in the original language. Contact your admin if you see untranslated content.
  </Step>
</Steps>

Pulse is currently available in German, French, Italian and English. Please note that your company might decide to use only a subset of these languages.

**Change your language preference**

1. Scroll down to the end of the page. On your bottom-left corner you will find the available languages
2. After clicking one of the languages, the active one will be underlined

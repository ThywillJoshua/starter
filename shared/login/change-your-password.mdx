<Callout variant="info" title="Applies to password logins only">
  If you use Single Sign-on (SSO) with your company credentials, change your password in your identity provider instead.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your profile">
    Click your name in the top-right corner, then choose **Profile**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit password">
    Scroll to **Password**, select **Edit**, and enter your current password plus a new one.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and confirm">
    Click **Save**. Log out and back in if you want to verify the change.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="warning" title="Password requirements">
  Minimum 8 characters and must include: one lowercase, one uppercase, and one special character (e.g., ?, %, /). Passwords with 12+ characters also meet the requirement.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="note" title="Find your company URL">
  Pulse always starts from `https://COMPANYNAME.start-pulse.com`. Bookmark it and share it in onboarding emails.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your company Pulse URL">
    Enter the URL in your browser: `https://COMPANYNAME.start-pulse.com`.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose sign-in method">
    If SSO is enabled, you will be redirected to your company login automatically. Otherwise, enter your email and password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Complete any prompts">
    Approve MFA or security checks if your company uses them, then you’ll land in Pulse.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="info" title="If you don’t know the company name">
  Ask your admin or contact Pulse support via the site’s contact link. Include your work email and company to speed things up.
</Callout>

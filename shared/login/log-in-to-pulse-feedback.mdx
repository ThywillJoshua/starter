To log in, you must go to your company's Pulse Feedback site. This URL looks like this:

```
https://COMPANYNAME.start-pulse.com
```

There you can log in with your email and password. If [SSO](https://www.start-pulse.com/en/resources/help/login-settings/what-is-single-sign-on-sso) is activated, you will be redirected to your companyâ€™s login screen.

If you are not sure about the company name used for Pulse Feedback, please [get in touch](https://www.start-pulse.com/en/company/contact) with us.

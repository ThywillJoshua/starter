Please note that guests have limited access to Pulse. Guests cannot participate in the surveys, but they can view the results.

**How to add guests to Pulse:**

1. Make sure that you are logged in with your admin account and click on your name in the top right corner.
2. Click on **Admin**.
3. On the admin page, locate the **settings** in the left menu and click on them.
4. On the settings page, you can see any guests you have added. You can add a **new guest** by clicking on the **plus symbol ( âž• )**.

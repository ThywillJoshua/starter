<Callout variant="info" title="Guest permissions">
  Guests can view results but cannot participate in surveys. Use them for auditors, coaches, or leaders who only need read access.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Admin settings">
    Log in as an admin → click your name (top-right) → **Admin** → **Settings** in the left menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review current guests">
    Check which guests already exist and whether they still need access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a guest">
    Click the **plus** (➕) symbol, enter the guest’s email, and save. Assign them only to the surveys they need.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove when no longer needed">
    Delete guest access after the review window closes to reduce exposure.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="warning" title="Data handling">
  Remind guests about confidentiality expectations before sharing results, especially if comments contain sensitive details.
</Callout>

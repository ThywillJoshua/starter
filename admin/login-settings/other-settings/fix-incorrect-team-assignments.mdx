<Callout variant="warning" title="Why this matters">
  Incorrect team/manager assignments can change who sees results and which surveys someone receives. Fix them before launching or releasing results.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Find the participant">
    Go to **Admin** → **Participants** (or **Participant management**) and search for the user.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Update team/manager">
    Edit the participant record and select the correct team and manager. Save your changes.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Confirm survey assignments">
    Ensure the participant is included in the correct active or upcoming surveys after the change.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Notify the participant">
    Let them know the mapping is fixed so they know where to expect results access.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="info" title="If you don’t have admin rights">
  Contact your company’s Pulse admin to make the update on your behalf.
</Callout>

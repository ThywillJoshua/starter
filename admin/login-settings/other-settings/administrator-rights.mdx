---

[Source](https://www.start-pulse.com/en/resources/help/login-settings/other-settings/what-rights-do-administrators-have)

Admins have full access to Pulse. They can manage participants and surveys and view the results. They can also participate in surveys if they are on the participant list.

The participant data are available for admins only in an anonymous form.

[Source](https://www.start-pulse.com/en/resources/help/login-settings/other-settings/how-can-i-add-and-manage-administrators)

<Callout variant="warning" title="Scope of admin access">
  Admins can manage surveys, participants, and visibility settings, and can view results. Only grant admin rights to owners who need broad control.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Admin settings">
    While logged in as an admin, click your name (top-right) → choose **Admin** → open **Settings** in the left menu.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review current admins">
    Confirm who already has admin rights to avoid duplicates or stale accounts.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add a new admin">
    Click the **plus** (➕) icon and enter the user’s email. Save to grant access.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Remove or downgrade (optional)">
    If someone no longer needs access, remove them or convert them back to a participant.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="info" title="Good practice">
  Keep a short list of named admins, rotate credentials when people leave, and use SSO where possible for lifecycle management.
</Callout>

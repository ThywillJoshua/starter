Pulse complies with the provisions of the EU Data Protection Basic Regulation (EU-DSGVO), as well as Swiss data protection laws. For more information on data protection, please contact the Pulse team (team@start-pulse.com).

<Callout variant="info" title="Compliance posture">
  Pulse aligns with EU DSGVO/GDPR and Swiss data protection requirements.
</Callout>

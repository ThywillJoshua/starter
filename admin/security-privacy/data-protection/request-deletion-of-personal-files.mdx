If we process your personal data as part of the Pulse Service, you have the right to request that we delete your personal data. Please contact team@start-pulse.com or contact the Pulse team of your company.

<Callout variant="info" title="How to request deletion">
  Email team@start-pulse.com or contact your internal Pulse admin. Include your full name, work email, and tenant/company name.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Submit the request">
    Send the deletion request with identifying details.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Verify identity">
    Be ready to confirm your identity if asked to prevent unauthorized deletion.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Track completion">
    Admins and Pulse will process the request and confirm when it’s complete.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="warning" title="Legal constraints">
  Some data may be retained as required by law or for legitimate interests, consistent with Pulse’s data retention policy.
</Callout>

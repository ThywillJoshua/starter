In addition to the provided question bank, you can also add your own questions.

**Adding your own questions**

1. Click on your name at your top right corner and go to **Admin**
2. In the Admin panel, click on **Questions** your left menu
3. Click on the **plus icon** ( âž•) located underneath the search bar to create a new question
4. Select a topic that fits your question
5. Add the question and the translations where necessary

**Note:** If a translation is missing, the question is shown in the base language.

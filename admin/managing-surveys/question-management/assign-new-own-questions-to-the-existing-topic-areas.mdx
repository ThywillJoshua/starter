Adding a question to a topic field is easy when creating a new question.

**How to add your own questions:**

1. Click on your name in the upper right corner and go to **Admin**.
2. In the admin panel, click on **questions** in your left menu.
3. Click on the **plus icon ( âž• )** located under the search bar to create a new question.
4. Choose a **topic** that matches your question.
5. Add the question and the translation if necessary.

**Note:** If a translation is missing, the question will be displayed in the base language.

<Callout variant="info" title="Assign topics at creation time">
  Topics keep custom questions discoverable and properly grouped in reporting.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Create a question">
    Admin → **Questions** → **➕** under the search bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick a topic">
    Choose the subject area that best fits the question’s intent.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Add text and translations">
    Enter the question and provide translations for enabled languages.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="warning" title="Missing translations">
  Without translations, the base language is shown to those readers. Fill gaps before launch.
</Callout>

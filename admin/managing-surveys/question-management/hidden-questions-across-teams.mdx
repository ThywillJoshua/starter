<Callout variant="info" title="Targeted questions">
  Some questions are only relevant to certain teams. Targeting keeps surveys shorter and results more meaningful.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Assign to specific teams">
    When adding questions to a survey, choose which teams should see each question.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Validate coverage">
    Ensure critical questions still reach all core audiences before launch.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="warning" title="Transparency">
  Communicate to managers if certain teams see different questions to avoid confusion when comparing results.
</Callout>

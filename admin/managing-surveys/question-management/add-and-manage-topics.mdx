**How to add topic fields:**

1. Make sure that you are logged in with your admin account and click on your **name** in the top right corner.
2. Click on **Admin.**
3. On the admin page, locate the **topics** in the left menu and click on them.
4. Click the **plus icon ( âž• )** located under the search bar to create a new topic box.
5. Add the topic field and the translation if necessary.
6. Click on **save**.

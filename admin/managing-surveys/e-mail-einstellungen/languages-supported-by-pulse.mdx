[Source](https://www.start-pulse.com/en/resources/help/managing-surveys/e-mail-einstellungen/which-languages-does-pulse-support)

Pulse is currently available in German, French, Italian, and English. Please note that your company might decide to use only a subset of these languages.

<Callout variant="info" title="Email behavior">
  Invitations, reminders, and results emails send in the participant’s selected language when translations exist.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Check enabled languages">
    Confirm which languages your company turned on in Pulse.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Provide translations">
    Add survey and question translations so emails and content match the user’s preference.
  </Step>
</Steps>

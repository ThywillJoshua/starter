[Source](https://www.start-pulse.com/en/resources/help/managing-surveys/comment-moderation/how-can-i-moderate-reported-comments)

Participants have the option to [report comments.](https://www.start-pulse.com/en/resources/help/team-feedback/how-can-i-report-a-comment)

<Callout variant="warning" title="Handling reports">
  Treat reports quickly to keep trust high. Document any edits you make.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open reported comments">
    Admin → **Comments** (left menu), then open the reported item.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review reporter note">
    Check the **Reported by** section to see why it was flagged.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Decide and act">
    Consult the reporter if needed. Edit the comment text in **I like** / **I wish**, or leave it unchanged if appropriate.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and notify">
    Save your changes; the commenter and reporter are notified that the comment was reviewed and what changed.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="info" title="See also">
  Guidance on handling inappropriate comments: <a href="/admin/managing-surveys/comment-moderation/best-way-to-handle-inappropriate-comments">read tips</a>.
</Callout>

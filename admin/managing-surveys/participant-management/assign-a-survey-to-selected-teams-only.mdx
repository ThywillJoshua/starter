[Source](https://www.start-pulse.com/en/resources/help/managing-surveys/participant-management/how-can-i-assign-a-survey-to-selected-teams-only)

<Callout variant="info" title="Target smaller audiences">
  Run pilots or focused surveys by limiting distribution to specific teams.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Set scope in setup">
    While creating a survey, find **This survey is open to …**.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Choose Selected teams">
    Pick the teams you want to include; leave others out.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Finish setup">
    Continue through questions and emails as usual; only chosen teams will see the survey.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="warning" title="Results visibility">
  Even if only some teams are invited, results may still be visible org-wide if permissions allow. Set expectations with managers.
</Callout>

**Assigning survey to teams**

1. While creating a new survey simply locate the last input field named **"This survey is open to â€¦"**
2. Select the option **"Selected teams"** and freely choose the team or teams to run a survey with
3. After choosing simply click on **Continue** and finish setting up your survey

**Note:** The survey will be shown to the selected teams only. However, the results can be viewed by everyone from your organisation.

**How to reuse a survey:**

1. Make sure that you are logged in with your admin account and click on your name in the top right corner.
2. Click on **Admin**.
3. On the admin page, locate the polls in the left menu and click on them.
4. Search for the survey you want to reuse. By clicking the " **Reuse**" icon on the far right of the page, you can reuse a poll.
5. Now enter all the new data you need (e.g. start and end date).

<Callout variant="info" title="How participants experience it">
  Emails contain a direct link to the survey with a short explanation of visibility rules. The same link works across devices and browsers; reminders can be scheduled for late responders.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Invitation sent">
    Participants receive an email (or intranet/Slack link) with a single URL. SSO users are auto-authenticated; others enter email/password.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Survey intro">
    Landing page outlines who will see the feedback and how anonymity works.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Answer questions">
    Typical survey: Pulse question plus 2–6 additional items. Each uses a 1–10 rating scale and two comment fields for context.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Submit and reminders">
    Participants submit; reminders go to non-responders until the end date. A single link routes everyone correctly.
  </Step>
</Steps>

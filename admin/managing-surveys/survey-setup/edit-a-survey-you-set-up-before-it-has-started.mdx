<Callout variant="info" title="Timing matters">
  You can edit all survey details until the start date. After it starts, only limited changes are allowed—see <a href="/admin/managing-surveys/survey-setup/make-changes-to-a-survey-if-it-has-already-started">changes after start</a>.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the survey">
    Go to **Admin** → **Surveys**, search for the survey, and open it.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Edit details">
    Adjust dates, audience (all or selected teams), questions, ordering, or email schedule as needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Save and recheck preview">
    Save your edits and use preview mode to confirm the experience before launch.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="warning" title="After start">
  Once the survey begins, question content and participants are locked. Only email settings and deadline extensions remain editable.
</Callout>

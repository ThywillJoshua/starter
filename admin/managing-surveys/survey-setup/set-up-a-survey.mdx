<Callout variant="info" title="Before you start">
  Have your participant list ready and confirm whether this survey targets everyone or selected teams.
</Callout>

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open Surveys and create new">
    Click your name (top-right) → **Admin** → **Surveys** → **➕** under the search bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set basics">
    Name the survey, choose start/end dates, and decide if it goes to all participants or specific teams.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Pick questions">
    Browse **All topics**, filter, and click **Use question** to add. You can target questions to certain teams if needed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Order questions">
    Default: Pulse question first, others randomized to avoid bias. Set a custom order if you need a guided flow.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Schedule emails">
    Configure invitation, reminders, and results emails (each sends once). Preview templates; content is fixed, but you can send manual follow-ups.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Review and add the survey">
    Use preview mode, then click **Add Survey**. It will open on the start date. You can edit or delete before it starts; after start, only email settings are editable.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Callout variant="warning" title="Link behavior">
  Invitations use a single survey link for everyone; only first-time non-SSO users receive personalized setup links.
</Callout>

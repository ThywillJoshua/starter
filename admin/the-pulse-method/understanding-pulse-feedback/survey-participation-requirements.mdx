Survey participation (i.e. answering and commenting on questions) is usually voluntary. Employees who do not participate should not be disadvantaged in any way.

<Callout variant="info" title="Participation stance">
  Participation is typically voluntary; non-participation should not lead to disadvantage.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="warning" title="Engagement">
  Encourage participation to improve data quality and outcomes, but keep it optional to maintain trust.
</Callout>

There’s no fixed frequency for Peer Feedback. You can conduct surveys whenever you want and with whomever you want. To find out more about Pulse’s usage in your organisation, contact your company's Pulse team.

<Callout variant="info" title="Typical cadence">
  Many teams run 2–4 peer cycles per year, but you can schedule as needed.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="warning" title="Prep peers early">
  Give participants advance notice so they can pick peers and plan feedback quality.
</Callout>

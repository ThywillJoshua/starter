There’s no fixed frequency for Team Feedback. Typically, two to four surveys are conducted per year, each with five to eight questions. To find out more about Pulse’s usage in your organisation, contact your company's Pulse team.

<Callout variant="info" title="Typical cadence">
  2–4 Team Feedback surveys per year, ~5–8 questions each.
</Callout>

<Callout variant="warning" title="Align with teams">
  Set expectations with managers and teams so they know when to prepare and follow up on results.
</Callout>
